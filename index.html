What's AI ?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a type of technology that allows computers to think and learn in a smart way. Instead of only following fixed instructions, AI systems can learn from data and improve over time. AI helps computers recognize images, understand speech, and make simple decisions similar to how humans do.
AI Is Everywhere
AI is part of our daily life, even if we don't always notice it. It is used in smartphones, search engines, video games, maps, and social media. When your phone unlocks using your face, suggests videos, or gives directions, AI is working behind the scenes to help you.
AI Awareness
Learning about AI helps us use it wisely and safely. AI can be very helpful, but it can also be misused or misunderstood. By understanding how AI works, we can avoid false information, protect our privacy, and make better decisions as technology becomes a bigger part of our future.
